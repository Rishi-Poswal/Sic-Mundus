What are the dates for Culrav 2023?

The 52nd edition of Culrav will be held from 12th to 14th April. Gear up for the 3 most awesome days of this year!

Why is registration necessary?

Registering on the website will generate a unique MI number for you. This MI number is essential for participation or entry to all the activities going on during Culrav If you want to register for a competition or accommodation or collect concert passes, youâ€™ll need to have an MI number and a college ID. Your MI number is essential to access the best things the festival has to offer.

How do I register for Culrav ?

Registration can be done in Registration page by filling in your personal details

Why should I give my information in registration?

All the details asked are relevant and are used to keep you updated about events at Mood Indigo. All the information is kept safe.

Is the entry free?

No

What to do after completing the registration?

After registering, you will get an email that will confirm your registration. Definitely keep up with our social media handles and regularly check your mail. After this, all you need to do is attend Mood Indigo :)
